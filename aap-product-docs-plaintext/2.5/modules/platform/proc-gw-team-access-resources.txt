# Providing team access to a resource

1. From the navigation panel, select a resource to which you want to provide team access. For example, menu:Automation Execution[Templates].
2. Select the Team Access tab.
3. Click the checkbox beside the team to assign that team to your chosen type of resource and click btn:[Next].
4. Select the roles you want applied to the team for the chosen resource and click btn:[Next].
5. Review the settings and click btn:[Save]. A progress bar displays indicating whether the role assignments were successfully applied.
6. You can remove resource access for a team by selecting the - icon next to the team. This launches a confirmation dialog, asking you to confirm the removal.
